FredBoat Improved Edition

FredBoat Improved Edition is the Discord music bot that is arguably 400% as good as any other music bot, given that it can play 400% as much music as other bots using our new advanced audio mixing technology (which we totally didn't copy from GitHub).

Why wait for other people's trashy music to finish playing when you can just add your own on top? Unlike FredBoat, the improved edition has no objections to clearing other folk's tracks.

Instructions

Invite the bot here

Example usage:

::help
::join
::play https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dQw4w9WgXcQ
::skip 2
::skip 2-4
::stop

The play command supports YouTube, Soundcloud, Bandcamp, Twitch, and various types of playlists.

You can grab the sauce from GitHub.
